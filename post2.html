City Continues Incentives for Seniors celebrating 80th and 90th Natal Day

  • Bogo City Government
  • Aug 30, 2024

The City of Bogo continues to honor senior citizens on their milestone birthdays. The birthday honorees were greeted with warm smiles and heartfelt congratulations from Vice Mayor Mayel Martinez and members of the City Social Welfare and Development (CSWD) Office, led by Amabelle Mayor.

The event is part of a city program designed to grant City Incentives to seniors who reach their 80th and 90th birthdays. This initiative underscores the city’s dedication to honoring its aging population and providing them with tokens of appreciation on these landmark occasions.

As part of the city’s birthday program, each senior received monetary incentives amounting to 5,000 pesos for those celebrating their 80th birthday, while those celebrating their 90th received the amount of 10,000 pesos, along with a personalized tribute cake. This program aims to encourage family and community support for seniors as they continue to thrive.
